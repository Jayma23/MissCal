Competition Rules

  • Eligibility: All UC Berkeley students are welcome to participate. A valid student ID or UC Berkeley email is required for verification.
  • Application Process: Participants must upload:
    • Preferred name
    • One amazing photo in JPEG or PNG format

    Optional details include GPA, major, personal story, clubs joined, and jobs or internships.

  • Voting: Voting is open to all UC Berkeley students. Each student is allowed one vote for their favorite participant.
  • Winner Selection: The participant with the most votes will be crowned Miss Cal and awarded a $1000 cash prize. In case of a tie, the organizing committee will evaluate tied participants based on creativity, confidence, and impact.

Data Privacy Policy

  • Privacy Commitment: At Miss Cal, we are committed to protecting the privacy of all participants. Your data is treated with strict confidentiality.
  • Data Ownership: Participants retain full ownership of their personal data, including photos and optional information.
  • Unauthorized Uploads: If any participant's photo or personal information is uploaded without their permission:
    • The incident must be reported immediately to support@misscal.com.
    • Upon verification, the unauthorized data will be permanently deleted from our servers.
  • Data Deletion: Participants may request the complete removal of their data at any time by contacting support@misscal.com.
  • Secure Storage: All data is encrypted and securely stored to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Verification: Login with UC Berkeley email is required to ensure authenticity and security.
