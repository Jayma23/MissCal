Competition Rules

Eligibility

All UC Berkeley students are welcome to participate. A UC Berkeley email is required for verification.

Application Process

Participants must upload:

  • Preferred name
  • One amazing photo in JPEG or PNG format

Optional details include GPA, major, personal story, clubs joined, and jobs or internships.

Tips for a Great Application

  • Choose a photo that showcases your personality
  • Share a unique personal story that sets you apart
  • Highlight your involvement in campus activities

Voting

Voting is open to all UC Berkeley students. Each student is allowed one vote for their favorite participant.

Winner Selection

The participant with the most votes will be crowned Miss Cal and awarded a cash prize.

The competition will have one winner determined by student votes. In case of a tie, the organizing committee will evaluate tied participants based on creativity, confidence, and impact.

Prize Breakdown

Base Prize: $500
10,000 Student Votes: $1,000 + Miss Cal Crown & Sash
20,000 Student Votes: $2,000 + Miss Cal Crown & Sash
40,000 Student Votes: $4,000 + Miss Cal Crown & Sash

Data Privacy Policy

Privacy Commitment

At Miss Cal, we are committed to protecting the privacy of all participants. Your data is treated with strict confidentiality.

Data Ownership

Participants retain full ownership of their personal data, including photos and optional information.

Unauthorized Uploads

If any participant's photo or personal information is uploaded without their permission:

  • The incident must be reported immediately to support@misscal.com.
  • Upon verification, the unauthorized data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

Data Deletion

Participants may request the complete removal of their data at any time by contacting support@misscal.com.

Sample Data Deletion Request

To: support@misscal.com
Subject: Data Deletion Request - [Your Name]

Hello Miss Cal Team,

I would like to request the deletion of all my personal data from the Miss Cal database. My details are as follows:

  • Name: [Your Full Name]
  • Email: [Your Berkeley Email]

Please confirm once the deletion is complete.

Thank you,
[Your Name]

Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my data?

All data is encrypted and securely stored using industry-standard protocols. We use SSL encryption for all data transfers and secure our databases with multiple layers of protection.

How long is my data stored?

Your data is stored only for the duration of the competition. After the competition ends, all participant data is automatically deleted within 30 days unless you request to keep your profile active for future events.
