Our Mission

Miss Cal is more than just a beauty pageant. It is a celebration of confidence, talent, and individuality. Our mission is to empower participants to showcase their uniqueness, inspire others, and create a positive impact in Cal communities.

Our Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in lifting others and creating opportunities for growth.
  • Inspiration: Encouraging participants to inspire confidence and positivity in their peers.
  • Integrity: We are committed to fairness, transparency, and respect for all.
