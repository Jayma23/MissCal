About Miss Cal

Miss Cal is not just another beauty pageant. It’s about finding someone who embodies the heart and soul of UC Berkeley—a person whose confidence, kindness, and leadership make her shine. We’re looking for someone who inspires others, leads with integrity, and brings out the best in Cal community. This isn’t about perfection; it’s about celebrating individuality—whether through personal stories, academic accomplishments, or the simple joy of connecting with others. Miss Cal represents hope, positivity, and the beauty of diversity, showing what it truly means to be a part of UC Berkeley.
